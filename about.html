Bio

My name is William Egge and I live in Virginia, near D.C.

My first hobby is video games. I have enjoyed playing games since I was a kid, but I also enjoy making them as well. I am not very good at writing compelling stories or interesting characters, but I love making fun gameplay loops that reward skill and dedication.

My second hobby is animation. I have never been very good at any form of art, but I recently started learning pixel art and simple animations last Christmas. I am still a beginner at it and I would say that I have a lot of practice to do before I create anything good. However, it is my favorite visual art an art form, given how much easier it is to control space and color.
